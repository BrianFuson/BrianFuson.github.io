More Work

I create a lot of work, like constantly. This is just a collection of things I like doing. Some are client and some are personal. For the illustrations, I mainly use my iPad for the linework and play around with how to color it. For photography, I love my Sony full frame as well as my little Sony RX100. I shoot mostly on 35mm film camera lenses adapted to my digital camera. Most of them have adapters, but I have designed adapters in Fusion 360 for the weird ones. The motion stuff is a mix of After Effects and stop motion, but I mostly prefer After Effects. Anything not in those categories are just fun things I wanted to feature on this portfolio that I did.

This portfolio website is coded in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by Brian Fuson.
